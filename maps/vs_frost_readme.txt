vs_bitten info
--------------
Time took to create:  on and off for a couple months.

Time took to compile:  about 30 mins.

Custom anything: A few custom textures & sounds...see below for more.

Special Areas: None, what you see is what you get.

Concept: I wanted something cold and dreary.  This map consists of a frozen valley populated with a few buildings, trees, 
and snow.


Other Maps: vs_bitten, de_lunar, de_bumrush, cs_lgn.  -All can be found at jmwrightdesigns.com
